Virtually anything you place inside these ovens will cook, and not just cook, but cook amazingly! Part of owning a wood fired oven is the joy of experimenting and creating your own delicious meals.

VEGETARIAN PIZZA RECIPE

Add the yeast to a small bowl with sugar and warm water (105-115°). Cover with saran wrap and let it sit for 5 minutes. In a medium bowl, add half of the flour, salt, and garlic powder. Stir to combine. Add the yeast mixture, oil, and gradually mix in remaining flour.

Knead for 3-5 minutes and transfer to a clean bowl. Cover with saran wrap and let rise until doubled by half, (30-60+ minutes), or overnight in the fridge. Gently punch it down and knead 5 times. Spread into a 12-inch circle and transfer to a preheated, floured pizza pan (or stone).

Use a fork to poke holes in the crust to keep it flat while baking. Brush with olive oil, then top with sauce.

Top with cheese and toppings. Bake in a preheated 450° oven for 13-15 minutes. Remove, slice, and serve!

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water (110°F/45°C)
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Activate yeast in warm water with sugar for 5-10 minutes.
  2. Mix flour and salt in a bowl, then add yeast mixture and olive oil.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for 8-10 minutes until smooth.
  4. Place dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let rise for 1-1.5 hours.
  5. Simmer crushed tomatoes, garlic, herbs, salt, and pepper for sauce.
  6. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C) with a pizza stone inside.
  7. Roll out dough, spread sauce, add toppings, then transfer to hot stone.
  8. Bake pizza for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese melts.
  9. Let cool slightly, then slice and serve hot.
  10. Enjoy your homemade pizza with your favorite toppings!

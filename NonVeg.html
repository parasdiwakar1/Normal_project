Grilled Chicken Escalope with Fresh Salsa Recipe

Chicken marinated in home-made spice powder and green paste. Grilled to perfection and served with a fresh salsa of grapes, spring onion and cherry tomatoes.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • For the Fresh Salsa:
  • 2 large tomatoes, diced
  • 1 small red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro (coriander)
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, seeded and finely chopped (optional, for spice)
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Pound the chicken breasts to an even thickness, about 1/2 inch thick, using a meat mallet or rolling pin.
  3. Drizzle olive oil over the chicken breasts and season with salt and pepper on both sides.
  4. Place the chicken breasts on the preheated grill and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until cooked through and grill marks appear. Make sure the internal temperature of the chicken reaches 165°F (75°C).
  5. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before servin

For the Fresh Salsa:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, chopped red onion, cilantro, and jalapeño pepper (if using).
  2. Squeeze the lime juice over the salsa and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Stir everything together until well combined.
